AboutEmzor Hesco

We introduce our company, Emzor Hesco limited, a subsidiary of Emzor Pharmaceuticals limited. Emzor Hesco markets products for surgery and other medical consumables. We have in our portfolio impressive list of international partners providing different solutions, Our overall focus is on providing your institution with Medical Equipment services and Tailor made solutions that will enable you provide sustainable quality services with our appropriate modern technology that meets your exact needs, which is coupled with skilled technical support and impeccable after sales service with manufacturer warranty.

We can offer you industry leading, complete theatre solutions spanning:

Endoscopy Systems
Cost Effective Solution from Aohua without compromising quality. It has a unique feature called Compound Band Imaging (CBI) which splits white light into components (Blue & Red) enhancing better visualization of lesions and blood vessels.
Theatre Equipment
This includes a varied range of high end, Anaesthetic Machine, Operating Table, Operating theatre light with integrated Ceiling Service Units, Diathermy machine etc., to enable you achieve maximum infection control and improve patient care.
Instruments & Orthopaedic Implants
By the industry leading manufacturer of Instruments and Orthopaedic Implants with outstanding quality and durability. Offer includes; Implants for surgical and interventional techniques, surgical instruments, power systems and a unique package of services.
Surgical Consumables
We provide different range of consumables, e.g, Sutures, Skin Staplers, Gastro- enterology, urology and consumables for non-volatile anesthesiological methods. Disposable Surgical packs Delivery and CS packs.
CSSD & Infection Control
Equipment for safe effective disinfection and sterilisation of instruments. This includes equipment ranging from Sterilizers, Autoclaves to Disinfectants.
