    About Us

    The group was founded by Dr. S. C. Okoli OON, an entrepreneur par excellence. Emzor Hesco Limited Provides solutions for Hospital Equipment and Services in conjunction with reputable manufacturers and brands from Europe, America and Asia.

    Our Mission

    Emzor Hesco Limited is a total health care solutions provider. We are a subsidiary of Emzor, a leading brand in the Health sector of Nigeria for the last three decades.

    Our Plan

    Emzor is a group of companies with interest in pharmaceutical manufacturing and marketing, retail pharmacies and logistics, as well as Medical Equipment.

    Our Vision
